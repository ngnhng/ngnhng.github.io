The quiet joy of small systems

Dec 26, 2025

Large systems invite heroics. Small systems invite steady care. I find myself reaching for the latter more and more, not as a constraint but as a gentle constraint that frees me from complexity tax.

A small system is legible at a glance. It reminds you of its shape, rewards you with quick feedback, and asks for only the simplest rituals to keep it healthy.

My favorite projects are the ones that fit in my head: a single page, a clear purpose, a few rituals repeated over time. The scale is modest, but the impact is wide.

That is the quiet joy: a system that stays friendly to the future version of you.
